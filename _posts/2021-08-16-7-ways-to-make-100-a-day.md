<br/><br/>

When you have nothing better to do, do you spend hours upon hours on your phone playing games? What if you got paid to do it?

Yes, you can! [Mistplay](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mistplay.mistplay&hl=en_US&gl=US) is a legitimate Android app that compensates you for your time spent playing. On Google Play, it has over 78,000 reviews with an average rating of 4 stars!

If you want to start producing money, follow these steps:

* Install the app.
* Add a game to your to-do list.
* You will begin earning units after 2 to 5 minutes of play.
* To gain extra units, provide developers with comments about the game.

Your feedback will be used by the developers to improve the games before they are publicly released. Once you've accumulated enough units, you can exchange them for free gift cards such as Visa, Amazon, Google Play, iTunes, PlayStation, and more.

You won't get wealthy by using this software, but it is a terrific opportunity to supplement your income by doing something you already do!

### **Become a Shopper**

If you enjoy going shopping, why not do it for others? You will operate as an independent contractor for **[Instacart](https://www.instacart.com/)**, delivering groceries for others. There's a lot of money to be made, especially right now.

During peak hours, you could earn up to $25 per hour, however, the average is between $10 and $20.

It's that simple: you've presented the order, you shop the order, and you're paid!

You set your own schedule and order what you want, and the flexibility is what makes it so appealing, especially if you're short on time, a stay-at-home mom, or a student.
