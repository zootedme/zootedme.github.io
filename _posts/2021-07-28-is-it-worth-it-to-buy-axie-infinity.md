### **What Axie Infinity?**

Axie Infinity is a [cryptocurrency](https://www.internetdailydeals.com/category/cryptocurrency/) gaming platform. Axie's objective is to make blockchain technology accessible to the general public in a pleasant way. It's an [Ethereum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ethereum) (ETH)-based play-to-earn cryptocurrency.

The Axie Infinity Shard token (AXS) and the other in-game token, can be earned in a variety of ways. In this Pokemon-inspired universe, players breed, nurture, and combat Axies to win tokens. Those who do not wish to participate in the game can purchase both tokens on decentralized markets.

Axies are cute digital pets, similar to [CryptoKitties](https://www.internetdailydeals.com/top-6-best-nft-games-to-earn-money/#cryptokitties), collectible digital kittens that started the NFT frenzy, and each one is unique. The cheapest Axies on the market today cost roughly $200, while the most expensive one went for 300 ETH or almost $550,000.  

The game's popularity, particularly in underdeveloped countries, stems from its earning potential.
