## **Why Do Companies Pay For Product Testing?**

<br/>

It's no secret that today's major and small companies rely extensively on product testing before putting them on the market for consumers to buy.

This technique allows them to determine the benefits, defects, and overall impact of their products, allowing them to enhance total sales.

<br/><br/>

## **The Best Companies to Work For If You Want To Get Paid To Test Products**

<br/>

It's critical to understand that there are two categories of products to evaluate:

* Cosmetics, food, beverages, technology, and so on are examples of **physical products**.
* **Digital Products:** such as game testing, app testing, internet testing, and so on.

The sites listed below are a mix of the two. Keep in mind that not all of them will compensate you for product testing. Instead, some of them will let you keep whatever compensation you receive. With that in mind, here are some of the best places to look for paid product testing opportunities.
