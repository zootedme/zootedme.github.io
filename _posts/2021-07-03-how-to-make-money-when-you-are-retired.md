You may have reached the end of your job and decided to retire. Alternatively, you may be ready to start a new business in addition to your existing work. Continue reading for simple ways to [make money](https://www.internetdailydeals.com/categories#Make-Money) while also teaching others what you've learned.



### **Resume Writing**

You may have already gained some essential lessons from your job. Assist in the writing and editing of resumes to assist others in getting started on their own career path. Charge $50–$75 per resume to supplement your side hustle income. Take it a step further and create premium resume templates to offer to your favorite customers.

- Tools: Scripting software or illustrator (optional)
- Time: Low to high
- Earnings: $50–75 per resume

### **Extra Driveway Space Can Be Rented**

It's possible that the extra driveway space you have is more valuable than you believe. You might rent out a driveway in a safe neighborhood if it is currently unused. Campers, boats, four-wheelers, vehicles, and trucks can all be parked. On a site like [Boxcar](https://www.boxcar.com/parking), you can earn up to $320 every month.

- Tools: Driveway, camera, or phone for pictures
- Time: Low
- Earnings: Up to $320 a month

### **Refurbish Furniture**

Refurbishing vintage furniture can be a lucrative side project as well as a lot of fun. You could establish a business if you frequently find used furniture to flip for your personal living space. Consider selling your flipped furniture on [Etsy](https://wwww.etsy.com), Facebook Marketplace, your social media channels, or your own website.

- Tools: Computer, refurbishing tools, camera or smartphone
- Time: High
- Earnings: Varies in earnings

### **Entrepreneurship**

In 2019, [13% of Americans](https://www.legalzoom.com/articles/13-retirement-business-ideas#infographic) aged 55 to 64 established their own business – it's never too late! You might create anything from an online store to your own media companies on your own own. Investing in your passions can keep you busy while also earning you money.

- Tools: Other tools depending on your interest
- Time: High
- Earnings: Varies in earnings

### **Rent Out Your Unused Living Spaces**

You could make money if you have unused space. Consider renting out your underused guest house, spare room, or even your entire apartment. You could make more than your mortgage or rent payments depending on your location, square footage, and features. [Airbnb](https://www.airbnb.com), Vrbo, and HouseTrip are just a few of the websites that allow short-term tenants to connect with landlords.

- Tools: Smartphone (for app usage and communication) and extra space
- Time: Low to high
- Earnings: $200–800 a month



[Working from home](https://www.internetdailydeals.com/categories#WFH) is an excellent method to supplement your income. You could be earning money while exploring the world or sipping your morning coffee in your pajamas. Whether you're searching for part-time or full-time employment, working from home is a terrific choice. Who knows, you might discover a new hobby or be able to pay off your credit card debt.
